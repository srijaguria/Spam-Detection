Founded in 2020 by a team of cybersecurity experts and data scientists, SpamGuard.io emerged from the growing need for intelligent email protection in an increasingly digital world. We recognized that traditional spam filters were no longer sufficient against sophisticated phishing attacks and AI-generated spam.
